Healthcare · Process Automation
Turning insurance chaos into same-day patient care

A high-volume ophthalmology practice seeing 5,000+ patients per month was losing up to $50,000/month in denied insurance claims due to a complex, manual verification process. Staff had to navigate 1,000+ insurance plans, each with different authorization rules and step therapy requirements — leading to errors, delays, and patients leaving without treatment. We built an automated system that consolidated all insurance contracts, procedure codes, and authorization rules into a single source of truth, enabling staff to instantly determine whether a patient could be treated same-day.
